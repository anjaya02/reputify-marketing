## 2. General Refund Policy

### 2.1 Monthly Subscriptions

- **No Prorated Refunds:** Monthly subscriptions are non-refundable once the billing cycle has begun.
- **Cancellation:** You may cancel at any time via **Settings > Subscription**. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

### 2.2 Annual Subscriptions

- **14-Day Satisfaction Period:** If you are unsatisfied with the Service, you may request a full refund within **14 days** of your initial annual subscription purchase.
- **After 14 Days:** Refunds are not available. You may cancel, and access continues until the subscription expires.

## 5. How to Request a Refund

### Step 1: Contact Support

Email **support@reputify.lk** with:

- Your registered email address
- Business name on the account
- Reason for refund request
- Transaction ID or invoice number (available in **Settings > Billing**)

### Step 2: Review

We will respond within **3-5 business days**.

### Step 3: Processing

Approved refunds are processed within **7-10 business days** to the original payment method via PayHere.

## 7. Subscription Cancellation

### How to Cancel

1. Log in to your Reputify account
2. Navigate to **Settings > Subscription**
3. Click **Cancel Subscription**
4. Confirm cancellation

### Effect of Cancellation

| What Happens             | Timing                              |
| ------------------------ | ----------------------------------- |
| Access continues         | Until end of current billing period |
| Auto-renewal stops       | Immediately                         |
| Data retained            | 30 days after subscription ends     |
| Data permanently deleted | 30 days after subscription ends     |

**To export your data before cancellation:**
Go to **Settings > Data Export** and download your mentions, analytics, and reports.

## 8. Plan Downgrades

- Downgrading does **not** result in a refund for the current period
- New pricing takes effect on the next billing cycle
- Feature access is adjusted at cycle end
- Team member limits are enforced at downgrade
