# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Effective Date:** [INSERT DATE]  
**Last Updated:** [INSERT DATE]

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs the acceptable use of the Reputify platform ("Service"). This policy protects Reputify, our users, and the integrity of online reputation management.

---

## 1. Purpose

Reputify provides AI-powered reputation management for businesses, including:

- Review and mention monitoring across 8 platforms
- AI sentiment analysis in 90+ languages
- AI-generated reply suggestions
- Fake review detection
- Competitor tracking

This AUP ensures the Service is used ethically, legally, and as intended.

**Note:** The platform interface is in English. AI sentiment analysis supports 90+ languages.

---

## 2. General Principles

All users must:

- ✅ Use the Service in good faith
- ✅ Respect the rights of others
- ✅ Comply with all applicable laws
- ✅ Maintain the authenticity and integrity of online reviews
- ✅ Comply with third-party platform terms of service

---

## 3. Prohibited Activities

### 3.1 Review Manipulation

You must **NOT**:

| Prohibited Action                               | Why It's Prohibited                                |
| ----------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| Create or post fake reviews                     | Violates platform ToS and consumer protection laws |
| Pay for or incentivize fake reviews             | Illegal in many jurisdictions                      |
| Submit false negative reviews about competitors | Defamation and unfair competition                  |
| Use Reputify's AI to generate fake reviews      | Misuse of AI for fraudulent purposes               |
| Engage in review exchange/swap schemes          | Coordinated inauthentic behavior                   |
| Delete legitimate negative reviews deceptively  | Manipulation of authentic feedback                 |
| Impersonate customers or reviewers              | Identity fraud                                     |

### 3.2 Platform & System Abuse

You must **NOT**:

- Use unauthorized bots, scripts, or automation
- Scrape data beyond authorized API access
- Attempt to circumvent rate limits
- Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble the Service
- Exploit security vulnerabilities (report them to us instead)
- Share account credentials with unauthorized parties
- Access other users' accounts or business data
- Create multiple accounts to abuse free trials

### 3.3 Harmful Content & Conduct

You must **NOT** use Reputify to:

- Harass, bully, or threaten individuals
- Defame or libel any person or business
- Distribute hate speech or discriminatory content
- Spread misinformation or disinformation
- Upload malware, viruses, or malicious code
- Share explicit, violent, or inappropriate content

### 3.4 Legal Violations

You must **NOT**:

- Violate intellectual property rights
- Breach confidentiality or trade secrets
- Engage in fraud, phishing, or deceptive practices
- Violate privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
- Use the Service for illegal activities
- Sabotage competitors' online reputation

## 5. AI-Generated Content Guidelines

When using Reputify's AI features:

### 5.1 Reply Suggestions

| Guideline                 | Requirement                                                         |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Review before posting** | Always review AI suggestions before publication                     |
| **Personalize**           | Customize responses to address specific customer concerns           |
| **Authenticity**          | Do not claim AI responses are from real individuals without editing |
| **Accuracy**              | Verify any factual claims before including them                     |
| **Language**              | Platform operates in English                                        |

### 5.2 Insights & Analysis

- AI-generated business insights are **suggestions**, not guarantees
- Validate findings with other data sources
- Do not make major decisions based solely on AI outputs
- Fake review detection provides **probability scores**, not certainty
